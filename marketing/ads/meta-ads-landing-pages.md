## Testing Strategy

### A/B Variables
- **Visuals:** Test pain-point vs. product-demo vs. outcome-focused imagery
- **Primary Text:** Test quote-based social proof vs. direct benefit vs. problem-solution structure
- **Headlines:** Test metric-driven ("< 24h") vs. benefit-driven ("Ship 10x Faster") vs. identity-driven ("Your AI...")

### Metrics to Track
- CTR (Click-through rate) - ad creative effectiveness
- Landing page → Book Demo conversion - message-market fit
- Cost per demo booked - efficiency

### Recommended Budget Split
- Support Agent: 40% (broadest audience, most validated use case)
- PM-Copilot: 35% (clear pain point, specific buyer persona)
- FDE: 25% (technical audience, may need refinement)

---

## Implementation Notes

1. All CTAs should route to respective landing pages with UTM parameters for tracking
2. Pixel events: PageView, ViewContent, Lead (on demo book)
3. Consider retargeting: visitors who viewed landing page but didn't book
4. Audience: Start with Lookalike from existing users, expand to interest-based (SaaS, Product Management, Software Engineering)
