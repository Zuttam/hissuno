# Hissuno Refund Policy

## Overview
Hissuno offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on all subscription plans. This policy applies to both monthly and annual subscriptions.

## Eligibility
- Refund requests must be made within 30 days of the initial purchase or renewal date.
- Customers who have used the platform for more than 50 support sessions during the refund period may be subject to a prorated refund.
- Enterprise plan refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.

## Process
1. Contact support at support@hissuno.com
2. Provide your account email and reason for the refund
3. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
4. Refunds are issued to the original payment method

## Exceptions
- Add-on purchases (extra seats, premium integrations) are non-refundable after activation.
- If an account has been suspended for Terms of Service violations, no refund will be issued.

## Contact
For refund inquiries, reach out to our support team at support@hissuno.com or through the in-app chat widget.
