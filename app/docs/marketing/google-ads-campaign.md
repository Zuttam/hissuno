# Hissuno Google Ads Campaign

## Campaign Overview
**Target Audience:** Product teams (PMs, Product Leaders)
**Value Proposition:** Speed to action - Turn customer feedback into engineering work automatically
**Campaign Goal:** Drive sign-ups for customer intelligence platform

---

## Search Theme Strategy

Google Ads campaigns work best with multiple themed ad groups targeting different search intents. Here are 5 complementary themes for Hissuno:

---

### Theme 1: **"Automated Feedback Management"** (Primary - 35% budget)

**Core Message:** Stop drowning in customer feedback. Let AI automatically triage, create issues, and build your roadmap.

**Target Audience:** Product teams overwhelmed with feedback volume
**Search Intent:** High-intent, looking for automation solutions

**Key Keywords:**
- [automate customer feedback]
- [customer feedback automation software]
- [automated feedback triage]
- "manage customer feedback overload"
- "automatic issue tracking from feedback"

**Ad Angle:** Pain point (overload) → Solution (automation) → Outcome (ship faster)

---

### Theme 2: **"AI-Powered Product Intelligence"** (20% budget)

**Core Message:** AI agents that understand your product and turn every customer conversation into actionable insights.

**Target Audience:** Forward-thinking PMs interested in AI tooling
**Search Intent:** Exploring AI solutions for product management

**Key Keywords:**
- [ai product management tool]
- [ai customer intelligence]
- [ai for product teams]
- "ai product feedback analysis"
- "machine learning product insights"

**Ad Angle:** Innovation (AI-first) → Differentiation (8 agents) → Results (smarter decisions)

---

### Theme 3: **"Feedback to Engineering Workflow"** (20% budget)

**Core Message:** Close the loop between customer conversations and engineering. Automatic issue creation, deduplication, and spec generation.

**Target Audience:** Teams with disconnect between support/product/engineering
**Search Intent:** Looking for workflow automation, issue tracking integration

**Key Keywords:**
- "feedback to engineering workflow"
- "customer feedback to jira"
- "automatic issue creation from support"
- [feedback to roadmap tool]
- "convert feedback into tickets"

**Ad Angle:** Integration (connects tools) → Automation (no manual work) → Alignment (everyone synced)

---

### Theme 4: **"Know What to Build Next"** (15% budget)

**Core Message:** Stop guessing at priorities. AI-powered analysis shows you exactly what features customers need most.

**Target Audience:** PMs struggling with roadmap prioritization
**Search Intent:** Seeking data-driven prioritization, roadmap planning tools

**Key Keywords:**
- "product roadmap prioritization tool"
- "know what features to build"
- "data driven product decisions"
- "customer feedback prioritization"
- "feature request voting"

**Ad Angle:** Uncertainty (roadmap chaos) → Clarity (data-driven) → Confidence (build what matters)

---

### Theme 5: **"Alternative to Manual Feedback Tools"** (10% budget)

**Core Message:** Like Productboard or Canny, but AI does the work for you. No more manual tagging, categorizing, or issue creation.

**Target Audience:** Teams using traditional feedback tools, frustrated with manual overhead
**Search Intent:** Looking for alternatives, comparison searches

**Key Keywords:**
- "productboard alternative"
- "canny alternative"
- "automated productboard"
- "feedback tool without manual work"
- "ai alternative to [competitor]"

**Ad Angle:** Comparison (like X but automated) → Time savings (10+ hrs/week) → Value (better results, less work)

---

### Search Intent Ladder (Across All Themes)

1. **Problem-aware** → "too much customer feedback" / "feedback overload" / "don't know what to build"
2. **Solution-aware** → "automate customer feedback" / "ai feedback analysis" / "feedback workflow tool"
3. **Product-aware** → "customer feedback automation software" / "ai product management tool"
4. **Comparison** → "productboard alternative" / "automated feedback tool vs manual"

---

### Campaign Structure Recommendation

**Campaign 1: High-Intent Automation (Themes 1 & 3)** - 55% budget
- Exact match keywords for automation seekers
- Dynamic keyword insertion for personalization
- High bids, conversion-focused

**Campaign 2: AI Innovation (Theme 2)** - 20% budget

- Medium bids, education + conversion

**Campaign 3: Strategic Solutions (Theme 4)** - 15% budget
- Prioritization and roadmap planning

**Campaign 4: Competitive (Theme 5)** - 10% budget
- Competitor comparisons and alternatives
- Aggressive bidding on competitor terms
- Emphasis on automation differentiation

## A/B Testing Strategy

**Phase 1 (Week 1-2):** Run all 4 variants equally, measure CTR & conversion
**Phase 2 (Week 3-4):** Double budget on top 2 performers, pause bottom 2
**Phase 3 (Week 5+):** Optimize winning variants, test new headlines

**Key Metrics:**
- CTR (target: >3%)
- Conversion rate (target: >5%)
- Cost per acquisition
- Sign-up to activation rate
