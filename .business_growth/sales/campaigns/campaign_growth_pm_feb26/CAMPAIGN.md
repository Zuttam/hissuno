## Sequence Design

**Important**: LinkedIn connection request notes have a 300 character limit. The full outreach message goes in Step 2 (after accept).

| Step | Type | Timing | Purpose |
|------|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Connection Request + Note | Immediate | Get connected, short personalized hook |
| 2 | Full Outreach Message | On accept | Deliver pitch + CTA |
| 3 | Follow-up | +5 days | Re-engage if no reply |
| 4 | Break-up | +7 days | Final soft touch |

```
Hey {{first_name}} - love what {{company}} is building. I'm working on something in the customer feedback-to-product pipeline space and would love to connect with a fellow product person.
```

### Step 2: Welcome / Full Outreach (on accept)
```
Hey {{first_name}}
Hope all is well
I'm working on a new agentic platform that turns customer feedback into clear, actionable product insights. It practically solves the product triage and broken data funnels problems and free up you and the team for deep dive product work

It still early days and would love to set a time to show you the platform and get some feedback.
Open for a quick call next week?
```

### Step 3: Follow-up (+5 days, no reply)
```
Hey {{first_name}} - just bumping this up. Would love 15 mins to walk you through what we're building at Hissuno. Happy to work around your schedule.
```

### Step 4: Break-up (+7 days, no reply)
```
Hey {{first_name}} - totally get it if the timing isn't right. If you're ever curious about automating the feedback-to-product loop, I'm around. All the best with {{company}}!
```
