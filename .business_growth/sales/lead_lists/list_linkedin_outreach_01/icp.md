# ICP Definition - LinkedIn Outreach Campaign

**Date Created**: 2026-02-20
**Campaign Focus**: Growth-stage product leaders

## Target Profile
- **Titles**: VP of Product, Head of Product, Director of Product, CPO, Senior Product Manager (at smaller orgs)
- **Industries**: B2B SaaS, Developer Tools, Platform Companies, Enterprise Software
- **Company Size**: 20-300 employees (Series A through Series C)
- **Funding Stage**: Series A, Series B, Series C
- **Geography**: Primary: United States. Secondary: UK, Canada

## Why Growth-Stage Companies?
- Large enough to have a product team + support team generating feedback
- Small enough that the feedback-to-product pipeline is still broken
- Scaling pain: manual triage, scattered feedback, support intel not reaching product
- Budget authority at VP/Head level, shorter sales cycles than enterprise

## Buying Signals
- Uses Intercom, Zendesk, Linear, Jira, Productboard
- Recently funded (last 18 months)
- Hiring for PM, support, or customer success roles
- Active community/open-source feedback channels (GitHub, Discord, Slack)
- Rapid user growth creating support volume

## Search Strategy
- LinkedIn People search: "VP of product" OR "head of product" OR "director of product" filtered by US + Tech/Software industry
- Company page People tabs filtered by "product"
- Targeted company-specific searches for curated growth-stage companies

## Outreach Messaging Template

### Initial Connection Request / InMail

```
Hey {name}
Hope all is well
I'm working on a new agentic platform that turns customer feedback into clear, actionable product insights. It practically solves the product triage and broken data funnels problems and free up you and the team for deep dive product work

It still early days and would love to set a time to show you the platform and get some feedback.
Open for a quick call next week?
```

## Notes
- LinkedIn free search limits: ~10 results per page, no company headcount filter
- Sales Navigator recommended for scaling this outreach
- Rate limits: ~30 profile views/hour, ~100/day, ~100 connection requests/week
