## Engagement Rules

1. **Vary reply types** - Not every reply needs an insight. Mix jokes, reactions, quick agrees with occasional longer takes. See STRATEGY.md Voice Guide.
2. **Be early** - Reply within 30 min of a post for algorithmic boost
3. **Match tone** - Adapt to the account's style (analytical for Shreyas, casual for Swyx, contrarian for DHH)
4. **No self-promotion** - Never mention products or link to anything in replies
5. **Be consistent** - 3-5 quality replies per day, every day
6. **Reciprocity targets** - Prioritize Tier 3 accounts for building actual relationships (they're more likely to follow back)
