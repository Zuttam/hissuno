## Engagement Rules

1. **Quality over quantity** - One insightful reply beats five "great point!" comments
2. **Be early** - Reply within 30 min of a post for algorithmic boost
3. **Add value** - Share a contrarian angle, concrete example, or deeper insight
4. **Match tone** - Adapt to the account's style (analytical for Shreyas, conversational for Swyx)
5. **No self-promotion** - Never mention products or link to anything in replies
6. **Be consistent** - 3-5 quality replies per day, every day
