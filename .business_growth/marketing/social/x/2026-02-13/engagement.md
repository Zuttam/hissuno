The productivity paradox of AI tools in engineering teams.

A thread.

[2/] AI makes you produce more output faster.
More PRDs. More specs. More code. More options.

But output was never the constraint.
The constraint was knowing which output mattered.
```

```
[3/] The bottleneck in every team I've seen:
Waiting for requirements that make sense.
Rebuilding what was already built because context was lost.
Shipping something nobody asked for.

AI doesn't touch any of that.
```

```
[4/] The teams that actually move faster aren't using AI to write more code.

They're shortening the distance between the person with the problem and the person building the solution.

Less telephone. More signal.
That's the real unlock.
