# Business Profile for Social Marketing

## Business Overview
- **Product/Service**: Customer intelligence platform that converts customer conversations into actionable engineering work. AI support agent powered by product/codebase knowledge, automatic issue creation and triage from customer feedback.
- **Company Name**: Hissuno
- **Website**: hissuno.com

## Target Audience
- **Primary Persona**: SaaS founders, product managers, and engineering leads at early-to-mid stage startups (5-50 employees)
- **Pain Points**:
  - Customer feedback scattered across support channels, Slack, spreadsheets
  - No clear pipeline from customer conversations to product decisions
  - Support teams know what customers need but that intel never reaches product/engineering
  - Feature request backlogs that nobody looks at
  - Manual triage of support tickets is time-consuming and inconsistent
  - Difficulty prioritizing what to build next based on real customer signal
- **Where They Hang Out**: r/SaaS, r/ProductManagement, r/startups, r/Entrepreneur, Hacker News, Indie Hackers, Product Hunt, Twitter/X

## Value Proposition
- **Main Value**: Automatically turns customer conversations into prioritized, actionable engineering work - closing the gap between support and product
- **Key Differentiators**:
  - AI support agent that actually understands your product and codebase
  - Automatic issue creation from customer conversations (not just tagging)
  - Connects customer feedback directly to engineering workflow
  - Knowledge compilation from codebase + product docs

## Messaging Guidelines
- **Tone**: Casual, founder-to-founder, slightly self-deprecating, honest about struggles
- **Topics to Promote**:
  - The support-to-product feedback loop problem
  - Feature request chaos and prioritization pain
  - AI-powered customer intelligence
  - Turning support conversations into product decisions
- **Topics to Avoid**:
  - Never pitch directly or drop links unprompted
  - Don't bash competitors
  - Don't make unrealistic claims
  - Avoid corporate speak
