## Engagement Rules

1. **Quality over quantity** - One insightful comment beats five "great point!" reactions
2. **Be early** - Comment within 1 hour of a post for algorithmic boost
3. **Add value** - Share a contrarian angle, concrete example, or deeper insight
4. **Match tone** - Analytical for Shreyas/Lenny, conversational for Swyx/Guillaume
5. **No self-promotion** - Never mention products or link to anything in comments
6. **Be consistent** - 5-10 quality comments per day, every weekday
7. **Write substantial comments** - 50+ words, with line breaks for readability
8. **Extend, don't echo** - Add your own observation that builds on the original post

---

## Daily Routine

1. **Morning check (Israel afternoon = US morning):** Scan notifications from target accounts
2. **First pass (8-10 AM EST):** Comment on 3-5 fresh posts with genuine takes
3. **Post your content:** Publish original post if scheduled for today
4. **Afternoon pass (12-2 PM EST):** 3-5 more comments + engage with comments on your posts
5. **End of day:** Quick scan for trending conversations worth joining

## Comment Templates (starting points, not copy-paste)

### For AI/Engineering posts:
- Share a production experience that validates or challenges the take
- "In practice, I've found that [specific observation]..."
- Add a dimension the author didn't consider

### For Product/PM posts:
- Bridge the PM perspective with an engineering reality
- "From the engineering side of this, the problem is..."
- Share a pattern you've seen across multiple companies

### For Founder/SaaS posts:
- Connect the business insight to a technical reality
- "This maps to something I've seen on the technical side..."
- Add a nuance from the CTO/builder perspective
